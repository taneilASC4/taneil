Characters

Mario

Mario is a fictional character in the Mario video game franchise, owned by Nintendo and created by video game designer Shigeru Miyamoto.

Luigi

Luigi is a fictional character featured in video games and related media released by Nintendo. Created by prominent game designer Shigeru Miyamoto.

Princess Peach

Princess Peach (JP) (formerly Princess Toadstool) commonly known as Peach, is a character in the critically acclaimed Super Mario series. In the series, she usually portrays the princess in distress, and has been kidnapped by Bowser in almost every game.

Yoshi

Yoshi, once romanized as Yossy, is a fictional anthropomorphic dryosaurus dinosaur who appears in video games published by Nintendo. Yoshi debuted in Super Mario World on the Super Nintendo Entertainment System as Mario and Luigi's sidekick.

Toad

Toad, known in Japan as Kinopio, is a fictional character who primarily appears in Nintendo's Mario franchise.

Bowser

Bowser or King Koopa is a fictional character and the main antagonist of Nintendo's Mario franchise. In Japan, the character bears the title of Daimaō.
